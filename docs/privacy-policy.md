# Privacy policy

**App:** Orbit
**Effective date:** 2026-06-11
**Contact:** In the app — Settings → About → Send feedback

---

## What Orbit is

Orbit is an Android app that helps you stay in touch with the people who matter to you.
It surfaces one contact at a time so the "who should I call?" question answers itself.

---

## Your data stays on your device

Nothing leaves your device. Orbit has no servers, no analytics, no cloud sync, and no
third-party SDKs that transmit data anywhere. Every byte Orbit reads or stores lives on
your phone and nowhere else.

---

## What Orbit accesses and why

### Contacts (READ_CONTACTS)

Orbit reads your phone's contact list so you can add people to your calling lists.
It reads names, phone numbers, and profile photos. It does not write to your contacts
or share them anywhere.

### Call log (READ_CALL_LOG)

Orbit reads your call history to show you when you last spoke with each person and to
calculate who you have not called in a while. It does not initiate calls on your behalf
and does not upload your call history.

### Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

Orbit can send you a quiet reminder when it is a good time to make a call, based on
the schedule you set. You can turn this off at any time in your phone's notification
settings.

---

## How your data is stored

All data Orbit saves — your lists, notes, and call history summaries — is stored in an
encrypted database on your device using SQLCipher. The encryption key is managed by
Android Keystore and never leaves your device.

---

## Deleting your data

To delete everything Orbit has stored, uninstall the app. Before uninstalling you can
export your data via Settings then Export, which saves a copy you control.

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be linked from Settings then About then
Privacy policy inside the app. A summary of changes will also appear in the changelog
in the GitHub repository at github.com/frosty110/friends-in-orbit.

---

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent from the app: Settings → About → Send feedback.
